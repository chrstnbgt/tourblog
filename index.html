BSCS EDUCATIONAL TOUR - DAY 1

On our first day of the industry visit, we went to Subic Bay Freeport Zone. We visited three organizations: PAGASA Weather Station, First Aviation Academy, and Vessel Traffic Management System. At PAGASA, the speaker, Sir Arnel Gonzales, discussed about weather forecasting. What interested me the most is how the coordination between the forecaster and the pilot works for the airplane to fly accordingly. At FAA, the speaker, Sir Dave Del Rosario, let us explore the inside of a plane used for aviation academies. It was also cool to see a analogue and digital type of planes. Lastly, at VTMS, the speaker, Sir Jerome Saddi, discussed to us how they manage their vessels. He also let us tour the room where people can monitor the waters.



BSCS EDUCATIONAL TOUR - DAY 2

On the second day of the industry visit, we visited first the QBO Innovation Hub. It is a place where start up companies can equip themselves and be the best version before being out in the public market. The speaker, Ma'am Karla Legaspi, discussed more how their company works and what surprised me is that companies such as AirBnB, Angkas, and Grab are start up companies. Then, we visited next the Packetworx Inc. - IoT Technologies Hub. The speaker, Ma'am Rose Ann Caraan, showed us different things they have created in connection with IoT. It was interesting to know that what we learn at school is really being made at the workplace.



BSCS EDUCATIONAL TOUR - DAY 3

On the third day of the industry visit, we visited the Light Rail Transit Authority - Line 2. The speaker, Sir Adrian Saldana, told us first the do's and dont's before enterning the manufacture or the repair zone of the trains. At the end of his discussion, we were able to really ride a train from Santolan to Antipolo then back again to Santolan. It was fun to be able to do it!



BSCS EDUCATIONAL TOUR - DAY 4

On the last day of the industry visit, we visited first the 98 Labs Inc. The speaker, which is also the CEO of the company, Sir Brian Tan Seng, tackled the essence of his company. He was very attentive with us and I was inspired by his story of determination eventhough he was not blessed financially back then. Then, the last company that we have visited is the Hytec Power Inc. The speaker, Ma'am Gretchel Listana, toured us their company and we saw different robotic stuff that they made that is beneficial to offices, schools, and houses. It is indeed fascinating how technology evolves and be impactful to a person's day-to-day life.
